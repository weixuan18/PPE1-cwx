   背井离乡 (背井離鄉)
   bèi jǐng lí xiāng
   Pinyin

Definition

   背井离乡 (背井離鄉)
    -
   bèi jǐng lí xiāng
    1. to leave one's native place, esp. against one's will (idiom)
